Finance ~ Banking

Before deciding to get into machine learning, I was an interest rates trader for European government bonds at Bank of America and at Goldman Sachs. I obtained my bachelor’s degree in Economics from Freie Universität Berlin in Germany. My Bachelor's degree Thesis was in PCA applied to the European term structure (European yield bond curves), answering the question of whether PCs would be affected by a negative interest rates environment. It was warmly welcomed and won 1st prize for best thesis of the year in my Econometrics department at the time, with a generous donation from the German Central Bank (Deutsche Bundesbank).
